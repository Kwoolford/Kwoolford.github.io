This site is a running log of the projects I'm working on and miscellaneous things I want to keep track of.

Most entries will be short digests auto-generated from project READMEs; some will be notes I drop in by hand. The homepage surfaces featured projects at the top, with the full archive filterable below. Each project page is meant to read like a notebook entry — a single-sentence summary, a stack row, a status, then sections for purpose, highlights, and technical notes.

If something is missing or broken, the source is on [GitHub](https://github.com/Kwoolford/Kwoolford.github.io).
